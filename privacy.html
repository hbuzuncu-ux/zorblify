Summary: Zorblify is a free browser game platform. You don't need an account to play. We collect minimal data, use cookies for analytics and advertising, and never sell your personal information to third parties.
01

Information We Collect

Zorblify does not require registration or login to use any feature of the platform. We collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, game interactions, device type, browser type, and general geographic region (country/city level).
  • Technical data: IP address (anonymized), operating system, screen resolution, and referral source.
  • Voluntary data: If you submit a leaderboard score, we store the player name you provide, your score, and the game identifier. No email or account is required.
  • Cookie data: We use cookies and similar technologies as described in Section 3.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the Zorblify platform and its games
  • To understand how visitors interact with our content
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To maintain global leaderboards and game statistics
  • To detect and prevent technical issues, abuse, or fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

03

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., remembering your cookie consent choice, local game preferences).
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand site usage. Data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior.

You can opt out of non-essential cookies using the cookie banner displayed on your first visit, or by adjusting your browser settings at any time.

04

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

Zorblify uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to Zorblify and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to Zorblify and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you visit our site, please visit: How Google uses data from sites that use our services.

Note: Zorblify products are ad-free in the sense that we do not allow advertisers to pay to influence game content or editorial decisions. Advertisements are served automatically by Google AdSense and we do not have relationships with individual advertisers.
05

Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect aggregated, anonymized data about how visitors use Zorblify. This includes page views, session duration, device types, and traffic sources.

Google Analytics data is processed by Google LLC in accordance with their privacy policy. IP addresses are anonymized before storage. We do not use Google Analytics to identify individual users.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

06

Leaderboard & Game Data

Some games on Zorblify feature optional global leaderboards powered by Supabase. If you choose to submit a score:

  • We store the player name you enter (which may be a pseudonym), your score, and the game identifier.
  • This data is publicly visible on the leaderboard.
  • We do not link leaderboard entries to any personally identifiable information.
  • Leaderboard submission is always optional — you can skip it and still play.

Game preferences (such as best scores and character selections) are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers.

07

Data Retention

We retain different categories of data for different periods:

  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months in Google Analytics (default GA4 retention period).
  • Leaderboard data: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.
  • Cookie preferences: Stored in your browser for 12 months.
  • Contact inquiries: Retained for up to 24 months for follow-up purposes.

To request deletion of your leaderboard entries or any data associated with you, contact us at contact@zorblify.com.

08

Children's Privacy

Zorblify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at contact@zorblify.com and we will delete it promptly.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's online activity. Game content on Zorblify is suitable for general audiences and contains no violent, adult, or inappropriate material.

09

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your data.
  • Right to opt out: Opt out of personalized advertising or analytics tracking.
  • Right to portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@zorblify.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of Zorblify after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
