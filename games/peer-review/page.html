About the Game

Peer Review is a management simulation where you build and run a research laboratory with one ultimate goal: winning the Nobel Prize.

Hire researchers with unique personality types, launch projects across seven scientific fields, navigate journal submissions, manage burnout, and outpace five rival institutions — all while keeping your budget in the black.
