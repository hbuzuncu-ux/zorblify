About Neon Runner

Neon Runner is an endless side-scrolling runner game with four distinct characters and themed worlds. Choose your runner — the zero-gravity Space Runner, the battle-hardened Ancient Warrior, the lightning-fast Shadow Ninja, or the desperate Last Survivor — and sprint through increasingly dangerous obstacle courses.

Each character brings a unique visual world: the Space Runner navigates a cosmos filled with meteors and laser beams; the Ancient Warrior sprints through crumbling Roman ruins dodging columns and boulders; the Shadow Ninja races through neon-lit streets evading shurikens and blade traps; the Last Survivor flees through post-apocalyptic streets littered with cars, fire pits, and razor wire.

The game uses a double-jump system, allowing mid-air course corrections that are essential for surviving the later difficulty tiers. At 400 meters, obstacles begin doubling. At 600 meters, threats appear from above and below simultaneously. At 1000 meters, wave attacks send clusters of obstacles in rapid succession.

A shop system lets you spend collected coins on powerups: Shield blocks one hit, Magnet attracts nearby coins, Slow-Mo dramatically reduces world speed for six seconds, and Boost grants a speed burst with temporary invincibility. Powerup strategy separates good runs from great ones. A global leaderboard tracks the top distance and score records for each character.
