⚡ VOLT GRID
Chain the energy · Clear the grid
⚡ How to Play
1
Tap the energized cells on the grid. The number inside shows the energy level.
2
The cell you tap discharges and distributes its energy to 4 neighbors (up, down, left, right).
3
If a neighbor reaches 4 energy, it chain-explodes too! Try to create a domino effect.
4
The level ends when the grid is cleared. Finish in fewer moves to earn more stars!
💡 Tip: Figure out which cell will trigger the most chain reactions. The right order changes everything!
